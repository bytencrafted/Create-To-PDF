1. General Information

The app Create to PDF allows users to convert photos and PDF files into a single PDF document directly on their device.

2. Data Collection and Storage

  • No personal data, photos, or files are uploaded or stored on external servers.
  • All PDF generation and file processing happen locally on the user’s device.
  • The app does not require user registration and does not collect analytics data.

3. Permissions Used

  • Camera: Only used to take photos for PDF creation. No images are sent or stored externally.
  • Storage: Required to access and save documents locally on the device.

4. Third-Party Services

The app integrates Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) to display banner ads. Google may collect anonymous identifiers in accordance with its privacy policy. More details can be found in Google’s Privacy Policy.
